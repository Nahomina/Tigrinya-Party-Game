Word Packs

What is Mayim?

Mayim (ማይም) is a word-guessing game. One team describes a Tigrinya word — without saying it — while the other team guesses. Each correct guess scores a point. The judge controls the timer.

What is Misla?

Misla (ምስላ) is a proverb completion game. A Tigrinya proverb is shown with some words hidden. Teams take turns trying to complete it. The judge reveals the answer and awards points.

How many players?

Best with 4 or more players split into two teams. One phone or tablet shared between all players is all you need.

Does it work offline?

Yes. After your first visit the game is fully cached and works without internet. Add it to your home screen for the best experience.
