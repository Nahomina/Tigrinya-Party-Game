Game Passes

What is Mayim (ማይም)?

A word-guessing game. One team describes a Tigrinya word — without saying it — while the other team guesses. Each correct guess scores a point. The judge controls the timer. Free Gasha tier included; unlock all tiers with the MAYIM Pass (£6.99).

What is Misla (ምስላ)?

A proverb completion game. A Tigrinya proverb is shown with some words hidden. Teams take turns completing it. The judge reveals the answer and awards points. Shared with MAYIM — one pass unlocks both.

What is Hito (ሕቶ)?

A trivia game about Tigrinya culture, history, geography and language. Play solo for a quick challenge or in teams for a group competition. Gasha (easy) questions are always free; Qol'a and Gobez tiers require the HITO Pass (£4.99).

What is Hinqle Hinqilitey (ሕንቅል ሕንቅሊተይ)?

The classic Tigrinya riddle game. The judge reads a riddle aloud and the team tries to answer. The judge reveals the answer and decides if it's correct. Gasha riddles are free; harder tiers require the Hinqle Pass (£4.99).

How many players?

Best with 4 or more players split into two teams. One phone or tablet shared between all players is all you need.

What is the All Games Pass?

For £4.99/month you unlock every tier across all 4 games. Best value if your group plays multiple games regularly — one subscription covers everything.

Does it work offline?

Yes. After your first visit the free Gasha content is fully cached and works without internet. Add it to your home screen for the best experience. Paid pack content requires a connection on first load.
