1. Who We Are

This Privacy Policy applies to the Tigrinya Party Games application, including Mayim (ማይም) and Misla (ምስላ), operated by Tigrinya Party Games ("we", "us", "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how information is handled when you use our Service.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or process:

  • Your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information
  • Account credentials (there are no accounts)
  • Payment information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or fingerprints
  • Usage analytics or behavioural tracking data sent to our servers

3. Local Storage

The Service stores the following data locally on your device only, using your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to us:

  • Your theme preference (dark or light mode)
  • In-progress game state (team names, scores, current round)
  • Cached word and proverb data for offline use (via Service Worker)

You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's site data or uninstalling the PWA from your device.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the internet. You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting google.com/settings/ads or by visiting aboutads.info.

For more information on how Google uses data from sites that use its services, visit policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

Advertisements are shown only on the landing page (index.html). No ads are displayed during gameplay. We do not control the content of advertisements served by Google.

5. Cookies

The Service itself does not set any first-party cookies. Google AdSense may set third-party cookies on the landing page for the purposes described in Section 4. Our Service Worker uses the browser Cache API (not cookies) for offline asset storage.

The Service is suitable for all ages and is designed to be played in family settings. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13 (or 16 where applicable under GDPR). Since we collect no personal data at all, no special provisions are required.
